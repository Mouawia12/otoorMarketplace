## Why Change Perfumes by Season?

Temperature and humidity significantly affect how a fragrance develops on skin. Choosing the right scent for the season ensures optimal performance and longevity.

## Summer Fragrances

### Ideal Characteristics

- Light and refreshing
- Citrus and fruits
- Aquatic notes
- Low concentration (EDT, EDC)

### Top Choices

1. **Acqua di Gio** - Giorgio Armani
2. **Light Blue** - Dolce & Gabbana
3. **Neroli Portofino** - Tom Ford

## Winter Fragrances

### Ideal Characteristics

- Warm and rich
- Spices and woods
- Amber and vanilla
- High concentration (EDP, Parfum)

### Top Choices

1. **Oud Wood** - Tom Ford
2. **Interlude Man** - Amouage
3. **La Nuit de L'Homme** - YSL

## Spring Fragrances

### Ideal Characteristics

- Fresh florals
- Green and herbaceous
- Balanced and light
- White flower notes

### Top Choices

1. **Flowerbomb** - Viktor & Rolf
2. **Mon Guerlain** - Guerlain
3. **J'adore** - Dior

## Fall Fragrances

### Ideal Characteristics

- Moderately warm
- Light spices
- Wood and leather
- Dried fruit notes

### Top Choices

1. **Spicebomb** - Viktor & Rolf
2. **1 Million** - Paco Rabanne
3. **Tobacco Vanille** - Tom Ford

## General Tips

1. **Layering**: Use lighter scents during day, stronger at night
2. **Occasions**: Work fragrances differ from social events
3. **Longevity**: Winter perfumes last longer than summer ones
4. **Refresh**: Reapply every 4-6 hours in summer

Remember: There are no strict rules - the most important thing is feeling comfortable and confident with your fragrance!
